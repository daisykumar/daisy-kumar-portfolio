Application Development: Designing and Coding Apps

Concept Description of App: A to-do list or task management app called CHECKLIST, developed in Svelte framework, built with Electron.

Used by those who need to manage and organise themselves at work. Belong to any profession or nationality but have one goal- to sort out cumbersome tasks, electronically, with a personalised to-do list.

Research

Function and App Idea: The app idea is to build a To-do List called ‘Checklist’ (revised name). Started with creating a ‘user-needs poll’ to figure out what’s commonly demanded by the users. Options sent to the users were:

To-do list app:

  1. Grocery app
  2. Time-tracker app with a to-do list
  3. Most votes went for to-do list app

User-Persona Description: The users of the app are those who work in an office or freelance, have lots of projects and clients to take care. They are looking for digital ways to organise themselves. They belong to different professions, and can be of any nationality. But all of them have one purpose- looking for task management apps to organise their work and projects.

User-Testing:

Feedback of positives experienced by users:

  1. Like the simply designed interface
  2. Envision the app as solving their daily problem at work of organising themselves

Features and interactions users like to see:

  1. Would like to have a calendar to better manage and remember tasks
  2. A softer purple color of the user-interface
  3. Would like to have a feature where they can mark tasks as 'complete'
  4. Want to see ‘default’ examples to figure out how to use the app

Key Takeaways:

  1. Agile process: Learnt practical approach of software development of products with agile method
  2. Built the ‘Minimum Viable Product’ version of the app to collect feedback from the users with minumum coding
